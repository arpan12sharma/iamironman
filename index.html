Elon Reeve Musk

visionary, innovative, and tirelessly dedicated entrepreneur

Here's a time line of Elon Musk's life until now:

  • 1999: Musk co-founded X.com, an online payment company, which later merged with Confinity to form PayPal. By 2002, PayPal was sold to eBay for $1.5 billion, netting Musk $165 million​.
  • 2002: Musk founded SpaceX with the ambitious goal of making space travel more affordable and sustainable​ .
  • 2004:He joined Tesla Motors as chairman and primary investor, helping drive Tesla's mission to promote electric vehicles. By 2008, Tesla introduced the Roadster, an electric sports car that marked a turning point for EVs​ .
  • 2006: SpaceX successfully launched its first rocket, Falcon 1, making it the first private company to reach orbit with a liquid-fueled rocket.
  • 2015: SpaceX landed a reusable Falcon 9 rocket, and Musk co-founded OpenAI to promote responsible AI development.
  • 2016: Musk co-founded Neuralink to advance brain-computer interface technology and merged Tesla with SolarCity to build a sustainable energy company​.
  • 2020: SpaceX became the first private company to send astronauts to the International Space Station (ISS), a groundbreaking achievement in private space travel.
  • 2023: SpaceX launched Starship's orbital test flight, setting a record as the tallest and most powerful rocket ever launched
  • 2024: SpaceX successfully conducted a fifth test flight of Starship—and managed to catch the first-stage booster in mid-air back at the launch site.
